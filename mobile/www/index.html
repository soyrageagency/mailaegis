MailAegis

Point this at your own MailAegis and you get the full client — mailboxes, verdicts, compose — on your phone.

Run mailaegis serve on a machine your phone can reach — your own domain behind HTTPS, or a LAN address like http://192.168.1.20:4850. Nothing is sent to us: this app only ever talks to the address you type.
